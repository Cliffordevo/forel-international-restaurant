Northern Nigerian Heritage

Hausa cuisine is characterized by its rich grains, aromatic spices, and hearty dishes that have sustained generations in Northern Nigeria. From tuwo to suya, each meal tells the story of a vibrant culture with strong trade connections across the Sahel region.

Traditional Refreshments

Complement your Hausa meal with kunun aya (tigernut drink), fura da nono (fermented milk), or our house-special zobo infused with ginger and cloves. These authentic beverages enhance the flavors of Northern Nigerian cuisine.

Authentic Preparation

Our chefs prepare Hausa dishes with traditional methods, using clay pots and open flames where appropriate to capture the authentic smoky flavors. We source spices directly from Northern markets to ensure the most genuine taste experience.
