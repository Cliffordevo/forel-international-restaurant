The Story Behind Igbo Cuisine

Igbo cuisine reflects the rich agricultural heritage of Eastern Nigeria, where yams, cassava, and palm fruits form the backbone of traditional cooking. Each dish tells a story of community gatherings, celebrations, and the deep connection to the land.
